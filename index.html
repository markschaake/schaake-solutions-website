FLEX Equipment Finance

FLEX Equipment Finance (FLEX) is a software as a service that provides equipment finance lenders with a one-stop origination, credit analysis, documentation, and contract servicing platform. Efficiently manage your portfolio with FLEX and reap the benefits of features such including:

  • Origination portal allowing your originators to enter deals and submit them for your review when ready
  • Credit analysis tools including PayNet and Equifax integration
  • Documentation generation and tracking, with DocuSign integration coming soon
  • AutoPay payment processing with automated emails to borrowers and lenders for all events
  • Portfolio analysis and lender cash flow reports.

About Us

Schaake Solutions was founded by Mark Schaake in 2016 with the mission to provide game-changing software-as-a-service solutions to equipment finance lenders. Mark has a background in both equipment finance and software engineering, having earned master's degrees in business administration and software engineering. Mark has work experience in loan origination, credit management, and has build end-to-end on-site software systems for lenders as an independent consultant. More recently, Mark spent time at startups in Seattle as a software engineer, and most recently as a Principal Software Engineer at the Allen Institute for Artificial Intelligence where he focused on web application development. With Schaake Solutions, Mark is using his software development skillset to develop state of the art web-based software for the equipment finance industry.
